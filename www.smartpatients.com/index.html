Smart Patients is an online community where patients and caregivers learn from each other about treatments, clinical trials, the latest science, and how it all fits into the context of their experience.

  • Use our built-in clinical trial search engine to find relevant trials and discuss them with the community.

  • Get help and advice from patients who have your same illness and similar complications or treatments.

  • Patients often become extraordinarily knowledgeable and get better care by learning from each other. You can, too.
