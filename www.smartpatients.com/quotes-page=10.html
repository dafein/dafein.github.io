  • Also, cancer is(at least in the case of a blood cancer such as POEMS) an immune response issue. Typically cancer cells(which tend to be created often, are usually readily identified and killed by other appropriate blood cells. When they are not identified it is because they have developed a capability to appear as normal blood cells to the rest of the blood cell community - and one current theory is that they have assumed(or developed) a messaging capability that tells other blood cells they are not a cancer cell - so the killer cells leave them alone and they(the cancer cells) can continue to proliferorate.
