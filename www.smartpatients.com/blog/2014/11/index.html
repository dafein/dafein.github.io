Sarcoma research in the last 20 years

November 22, 2014Roni Zeiger

Have a look at the graph above representing the evolution of sarcoma trials during the last 20 years. 1995 was significant. We were building the first ACOR online communities and the rapid growth in the number of clinical trials accepting sarcoma patients had begun. However, as you can see, relatively few of the phase 1 and phase 2 trials were followed by phase 3 trials, which are the ones that lead to approval of new drugs.

Medical professionals can now “Learn from Smart Patients”

November 14, 2014Roni Zeiger

We’ve had an increasing number of doctors and other clinicians asking if they can join Smart Patients to learn from what patients are talking about. While many members welcome this, others tell us they want to keep the community as a space for just patients and caregivers. At the same time, they strongly support the idea of the healthcare system learning from them.

So we’re launching Learn from Smart Patients, a new website and a new program where we are working with medical centers and training programs, to create learning materials for them based on conversations in Smart Patients. Curated conversations are de-identified and used with patient permission.
