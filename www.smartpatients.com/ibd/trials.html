Clinical Trials for Inflammatory Bowel Disease

Medical researchers perform clinical trials to see if new treatments really work before using them on large groups of people. Some clinical trials help researchers understand which x-ray tests work best or why certain people get a particular disease and others don’t. There are many clinical trials going on throughout the world about inflammatory bowel disease although most are focused on adults.

Below are some of the clinical trials in the US that are currently accepting children with inflammatory bowel disease. They’ve been organized into groups to help you find the trials that are most interesting to you.

If you’d like to search the entire database of clinical trials, including those in adults and in other countries, you can do so at https://www.smartpatients.com/trials.
