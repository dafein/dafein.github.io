Janet's Story

Janet Freeman-Daily

Writer

Janet Freeman-Daily is a writer, science geek, and metastatic lung cancer (LC) patient. A retired aerospace engineer with MIT and Caltech degrees, she employs her skills to translate the experience and science of lung cancer treatment and research into language other patients can understand. Her days are spent tracking research and treatments, supporting others in online forums, raising awareness through writing, blogging and public speaking, and participating in a clinical trial.

Janet is a founder and moderator of Lung Cancer Social Media (#LCSM) tweetchats and collaborates on projects with lung cancer nonprofits. She blogs at http://grayconnections.wordpress.com and tweets as @JFreemanDaily.
